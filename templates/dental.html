Dental Insurance

  1. Home
  2. Dental

Dental insurance policy is a type of health insurance that covers your dental expenses when you need oral care. Dental insurance policy can help you prevent and treat dental problems, such as cavities, gum disease, and tooth loss. Dental insurance policy can also improve your smile and your overall health.

The policy is ideal for you if you have Medicare health insurance that doesn't provide dental coverage, youâ€™re not covered through an employer, you've recently retired and now find yourself without dental coverage or simply your current dental policy isn't meeting your needs.

Depending on the policy you choose, dental insurance policy can offer different benefits and features, such as:

  1. Preventive care: This covers the costs of routine dental check-ups, cleanings, and x-rays. Preventive care can help you avoid more serious and costly dental issues in the future.
  2. Basic care: This covers the costs of common dental procedures, such as fillings, extractions, and root canals.
  3. Major care: This covers the costs of complex dental procedures, such as crowns, bridges, implants, and dentures. Major care can help you replace missing or damaged teeth and enhance your appearance.
  4. Orthodontic care: This covers the costs of braces and other devices that correct the alignment of your teeth and bite. Orthodontic care can help you improve your smile and your oral health.

Dental insurance policy can vary in terms of the amount of coverage, the network of dentists, the co-payments, the deductibles, and the exclusions. It is important to compare different policies and choose the one that best suits your needs and budget.
